ABOUT RISHABH CABLES

Established in the year 1975, We Rishabh Cables(INDIA) PRIVATE LIMITED. is known to be amongst the eminent manufacturer of these highly laudable ranges of Cables. Rishabh cables give a wide range of Electrical Wires and Electrical Cables. Manufacturing of this range is done using the supreme quality materials and modern machines as per the standards set by the industry. Our products are praised for their performance, long service life, strength, precision. Further, the range is punctually delivered and decently priced.

Providing superior customer support and service for over 2 decades has been over success mantra on which we are proud of giving the best handholding to our consumers whenever they need. Our team of machining experts works as a unit to provide unique solutions to difficult projects, precisely and efficiently to meet the deadlines.

Owing to our sound infrastructure and talented workforce, we have been able to fulfill the requirements of the products on time. Backed by a well-connected distribution network, we value people's time, and we have been capable of delivering the product safely at the client place within the promised frame of time. Our team works in very close coordination in a pattern with each other to accomplish set production targets of the organization. Strict quality checking is performed after every step, over the entire range by our specialized team of quality controllers, who assure us to make the supply of error-free products in the market to optimize resources in a most efficient manner and reduce minimum wastage.
